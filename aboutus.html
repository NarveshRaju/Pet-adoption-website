About PetConnect

At PetConnect, we believe that every pet deserves a loving home. Our mission is to connect prospective pet parents with shelters and rescues to make pet adoption as seamless and joyful as possible. With a network of over 14,500 shelters and rescues, we aim to make it easy for you to find the perfect furry (or feathered!) companion.

Our Story

Founded by a group of passionate animal lovers, PetConnect started with a simple goal: to help animals in need find forever homes. Since our founding, we have worked with communities, shelters, and volunteers to expand our reach and impact. We are dedicated to providing trustworthy connections and guidance throughout the adoption process.

Our Mission & Values

  • Compassion: We believe every animal deserves care and respect.
  • Commitment: We are committed to helping you find a pet that matches your lifestyle and needs.
  • Community: Our strength lies in the network of shelters, volunteers, and supporters that share our vision.

Why Choose PetConnect?

With PetConnect, you’re not just adopting a pet—you’re saving a life and becoming part of a caring community. Our easy-to-use search tool and dedicated support make pet adoption convenient, whether you’re looking for a dog, cat, or other companion.
